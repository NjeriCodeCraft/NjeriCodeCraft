```
She codes. She analyzes. She builds. She ships. ✨
```

I'm **Faith Njeri** — a Computer Science & Mathematics graduate (Zetech University, November 14, 2025) who believes technology is most powerful when it solves *real* problems for *real* people.

Right now I'm a **Data Science Fellow at Tech4Dev (Women Techsters)**, going deep on analytics, ML, and visualization — while continuing to build full-stack MERN applications that serve communities across Africa.

## 🚀 Featured Projects

### ♻️ [WasteLink — Image Validation API](https://github.com/NjeriCodeCraft/WasteLink-Image-Validation-API)
**`Tech4Dev Women Techsters Capstone Project`**

A FastAPI + TensorFlow ML service that classifies waste images into 5 categories in real time — powering a mobile waste management app. Built to make recycling smarter across African cities.

`Python` `FastAPI` `TensorFlow` `MobileNetV2` `Render`

---

### 🌾 [AgriScore-Pay](https://github.com/NjeriCodeCraft/agriscore-pay)
**`Hackathon Project`**

AI-powered agricultural credit scoring and input marketplace. Predictive yield modeling + Interswitch APIs to bring data-driven financing to smallholder farmers.

`JavaScript` `Node.js` `AI/ML Integration` `Interswitch APIs`

---

### 🏘️ [RentRadar](https://github.com/NjeriCodeCraft/rent-radar)
**`PLP Capstone Project`**

Full-stack MERN rental platform with smart filtering, location-based search, and agent services — built for budget-conscious tenants.

`MongoDB` `Express.js` `React.js` `Node.js` `REST APIs`

---

### 📅 [FundiFix — Booking Bot](https://github.com/NjeriCodeCraft/Booking_Bot) 🏆 *3rd Place*
**`Vibe Coding Hackathon 2.0`**

AI-powered scheduling assistant for local fundis and freelancers — managing client appointments through a conversational Telegram interface.

`Python` `Telegram Bot API` `Automation Logic`

---

### 🤖 [AI Resume Matcher](https://github.com/NjeriCodeCraft/ai-resume-matcher)
